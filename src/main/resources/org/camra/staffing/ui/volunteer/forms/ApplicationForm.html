We may assign you to work in any Areas which you have selected Yes or Don't Mind, although we will try our best to assign you mainly to your Yes Areas. We will not assign you to No areas unless we are desperately short and you agree. Choose Your Sessions

You may or may not be assigned work on all the sessions you indicate, it depends on how many volunteers are already assigned at those times. We will let you know exactly which session we need you for closer to the Festival.

In order to try and avoid volunteer overcrowding at quiet times and being short-staffed at busy times, the sessions are colour coded to indicate roughly how many more staff are needed.
